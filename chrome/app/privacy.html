Privacy Policy

Here's the informal policy. As far as the topic of "you" is concerned, I am Jon Snow.

I will NEVER collect personal information about you (for example: Github username, bookmarks) or what content you browse.

I reserve the right to collect the following in future (though I don't do it right now):

  • What features of the extension you use (eg. are you using bookmarks? Github repos?). Knowing what features of the extension you use will help me improve it.
  • Whatever Google Analytics tracks by default (geo, browser plugins, time spent on page). This will still only track usage of the extension itself; not your browsing.
