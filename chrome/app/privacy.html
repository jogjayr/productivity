Privacy Policy

I don't have a formal policy right now, so here's the informal one. As far as the topic of "you" is concerned, I am Jon Snow.

I store no personal information about you (for example: Github username, bookmarks etc.) or what content of yours you choose to browse.

I reserve the right to track the following in future (though I don't do it right now):

  • What features of the app you use (eg. are you using bookmarks? Github repos?). To re-iterate, I will know nothing about your content. Knowing what features of the extension you use will help me improve it.
  • Whatever Google Analytics tracks by default (geo, browser plugins, time spent on page)
