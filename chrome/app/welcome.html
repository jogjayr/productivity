Welcome to Browse Healthy!

Browse Healthy helps you improve your web browsing habits. Less empty-calorie social media, meaningless news and discussion forums. More nutritious, worthwhile, meaningful web experiences.

Here's how it works:

  • Select a few sources of productive distraction
  • When you open a new tab, Browse Healthy wil offer you a few suggestions
  • If you aren't interested in something, click on the red X and it won't be shown again
