Welcome to Browse Healthy!

Browse Healthy helps you improve your web browsing habits. Less empty-calorie social media, meaningless news and discussion forums. More nutritious, worthwhile, meaningful web experiences.

Browse Healthy can work in two ways:

  • Distraction mode - Displays useful suggestions curated from your Github starred repos, bookmarks, Keep and Pocket notes (coming soon!) when you're opening a new tab
  • Ironman mode - Detects when you're about to reach for an unhealthy snack (Facebook, Reddit et al) and reminds you of all the other, better, equally delicous options. Use this when you really need to concentrate. (Note: requires advanced permissions)
